About Me

Doctoral student in theoretical & computational astrophysics at the Laboratory for Galaxy Evolution and Spectral Modelling, École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland.

My research involve modelling & running numerical simulations of massive black hole accretion & feedback in idealised galaxies, more specifically in the low-metallicity dwarf galaxy regime.
