Jonathan Petersson

Soon-to-be PhD student in astrophysics at the École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), Switzerland, where I will be working on galaxy evolution and spectral modeling, with a focus on spectral signatures for black hole physical processes in high-resolution hydrodynamical simulations. Prior to this, I was a master student in astrophysis at Lund Observatory, Sweden, working on star formation in shell galaxies.
