Jonathan Petersson

PhD student in astrophysics at the Laboratory for Galaxy Evolution and Spectral Modelling (GALSPEC), École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL).

My research involves numerical simulations of BH accretion, AGN feedback, emission-line signatures of SMBHs and related feedback processes, all within the context of galaxy formation and evolution (especially at high-redshift).
