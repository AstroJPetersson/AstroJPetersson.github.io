Jonathan Petersson

PhD student in astrophysics at École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), Switzerland. My research involves galaxy evolution and spectral modeling, with a focus on spectral signatures for black hole physical processes in high-resolution hydrodynamic simulations. Prior to this, I was a master student in astrophysis at Lund Observatory, Sweden, working on star formation in shell galaxies.
