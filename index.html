About Me

Doctoral student in theoretical & computational astrophysics at the Laboratory for Galaxy Evolution and Spectral Modelling, École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland.

My research involve modelling & running numerical simulations of gas accretion onto MBHs in galaxy centres & AGN feedback, all within the context of galaxy formation and evolution (especially at high-redshift).
