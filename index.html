About Me

Doctoral student in theoretical and computational astrophysics at the Laboratory for Galaxy Evolution and Spectral Modelling, École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland.

My research involve modelling and running numerical simulations of massive black hole accretion and feedback in idealised galaxies, more specifically in the low-mass dwarf galaxy regime.

Prior to my PhD, I did my BSc and MSc at Lund Univeristy, Sweden.

Originally from Älmhult, Sweden.
